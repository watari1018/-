# Audio Sources

All current ambience files are local MP3 previews from Mixkit free sound effects.
They are used as temporary ambience samples and can be replaced later by指定 BGM
or preferred ambience files.

- `audio/crowd.mp3`: Mixkit "Shopping center crowd"
- `audio/train-interior.mp3`: Mixkit "Subway interior ambience"
- `audio/train-station.mp3`: Mixkit "Train station ambience"
- `audio/rain.mp3`: Mixkit "Driving in the rain"
- `audio/wind-snow.mp3`: Mixkit "Wind blowing ambience"
- `audio/night-wind.mp3`: Mixkit "Wind in the top of the mountain"
- `audio/danger-wind.mp3`: Mixkit "Scary graveyard wind"
